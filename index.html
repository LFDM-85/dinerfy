About Us - WHY CHOOSE US?

"A great restaurant is one that just makes you feel like you're not sure whether you went out or you came home."

A quality and relaxing rural restaurant, run by award winning owners, in the beautiful Barra village of Aveiro just a short drive from the A25. The building and decor are charming and rustic, with elegant modern touches. Relax by the cozy log burner perfect for winter evenings or enjoy outdoor seating overlooking one of the most beautiful beaches in Portugal in the summer months. A daily menu, all freshly prepared in the kitchen, includes fresh fish, pies, steaks and chef's daily specials with main course prices ranging from 4€ - 11€. As all dishes are prepared in the kitchen, they are able to cater for most dietary needs, with most sauces, soups and gravy all created gluten free. The Dinnerfy also offers gluten free bread and gluten free Yorkshire puddings on a Sunday.
